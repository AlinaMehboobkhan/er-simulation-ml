# Scenario Analysis

1. Baseline (5 doctors, 8 patients/hr)
2. Mass Casualty (5 doctors, 16 patients/hr)
3. Doctor Shortage (3 doctors, 8 patients/hr)
4. Optimal Staffing (8 doctors, 8 patients/hr)
5. Night Shift (4 doctors, 4 patients/hr)

## Recommendation
Keep at least 6 doctors during peak hours to maintain wait times under 30 minutes.
